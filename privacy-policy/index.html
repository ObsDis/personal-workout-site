Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the BarbellMind application ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information. The App is operated by Obsidian Distribution LLC ("we", "us").

Data we collect

When you create an account and use the App, we collect:

  • Account data: your email address, used to identify your account and send sign-in emails.
  • Workout data: exercises, sets, reps, weights, and other entries you log.
  • Body metrics: body weight, measurements (waist, chest, biceps, thighs, calves), and progress photos you upload.
  • Nutrition data: food entries (name, brand, macros, barcode, optional photo) and meal plans you create.
  • AI usage data: messages you send to the optional AI assistant, sent to our AI providers only when you initiate a chat.
  • Subscription data: subscription status and transaction history managed by Apple and RevenueCat.

We do not collect your location, your contacts, browsing history outside the App, or any third party tracking IDs (no IDFA, no fingerprinting).

How we use your data

  • Provide the App features, such as storing your workouts and displaying your history.
  • Process your subscription.
  • Improve the AI assistant by reviewing aggregated, anonymized usage patterns.

We do not sell your data to third parties.

Where your data is stored

  • Supabase (PostgreSQL, United States): account, workout, nutrition, and measurement data, protected by row level security so only you can access your rows.
  • Supabase Storage: progress photos, in a private bucket with short lived signed URLs.
  • Apple StoreKit: subscription transaction history, handled by Apple.
  • RevenueCat: subscription status and webhook events. It does not see your workout or health data.
  • AI providers: text and image content you submit to the AI assistant. We send only what you explicitly send.

Your rights

  • Delete your account by emailing lev@obsidiandist.com. We permanently delete all your data within 30 days.
  • Export your data by emailing the same address. We provide a JSON export within 14 days.
  • Cancel your subscription via your Apple ID account settings.

Children

The App is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The date above reflects changes, and material changes will be announced inside the App.
