Features

Built to train and eat with intent

Everything BarbellMind does, in one place. No spreadsheets, no guesswork.

🏋

Workout planning

Build any split: push pull legs, upper lower, full body, or your own. Each day gets its own routine. Reorder with drag and drop, add exercises mid session, and log sets with smart presets.

💪

PR and history tracking

See your last session heaviest and lifetime PR for every lift, plus volume history per muscle group over time.

📷

Macro tracking, four ways

AI photo recognition, barcode scanning, a food search across large databases, or manual entry. Saved foods make repeats instant.

🍳

Meal plans and shopping lists

Build multi day meal plans, generate a shopping list of everything to buy, and log a planned meal in one tap. A meal plan does not count toward your macros until you log it.

⚡

AI coach

Ask for advice by text or voice. Hands free voice mode works mid workout. The coach can log food, sets, and body weight for you.

❤

Apple Health

Steps, move, exercise minutes, sleep, heart rate, and body weight on your dashboard, matched to your Apple Fitness goals and rings.

📷

Progress photos and measurements

Track body weight, waist, chest, arms, and legs, and keep progress photos to compare over time.

🤖

Runs from Claude and ChatGPT

BarbellMind connects to AI assistants over MCP, so you can log and plan from a chat. See how.

☀

Home screen widgets

Today macros and today workout at a glance, right on your home screen.
