Claude

Connect in Claude

Web or desktop.

1

Open connectors

Go to Settings, then Connectors, then Add custom connector.

2

Paste the URL

Paste the connection URL above and continue. Claude finds the BarbellMind sign in automatically.

3

Sign in and approve

Sign in with your BarbellMind account on the consent screen and approve. The tools appear in your chat.

ChatGPT

Connect in ChatGPT

Developer Mode is available on Plus, Pro, Business, Enterprise, and Edu plans.

1

Turn on Developer Mode

Go to Settings, then Connectors, then Advanced, and switch on Developer mode.

2

Add the connector

Add a custom connector and paste the same connection URL above.

3

Sign in and approve

Sign in and approve access. ChatGPT stays connected with secure tokens, so you only do this once.
