FAQ

Good to know

What is BarbellMind? +

An AI training and nutrition app. It plans your workouts, tracks your macros and lifts, syncs Apple Health, and gives guidance based on your own data.

What does "run it from Claude or ChatGPT" mean? +

BarbellMind exposes a secure connector so an AI assistant can read and update your data with your permission. Log a meal or workout, build a plan, or check progress by asking, then open the app for the full view. See the AI Coach page.

Is it free? +

BarbellMind is a subscription with a free trial. Plans are shown clearly before you sign up.

Do I need the app, or can I use the website? +

The full experience lives in the iOS app. You can sign in on the web at the login page to manage your account.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription? +

Subscriptions are billed through Apple, so you manage or cancel them in your iPhone Settings under your Apple ID. See Manage Subscription for steps.

Is there an Android version? +

iOS first. Android is on the roadmap.

How is my data handled? +

Your data is private to your account and never sold. Read the full Privacy Policy.
