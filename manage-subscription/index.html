Account

Manage your subscription

BarbellMind subscriptions are billed by Apple, so you change or cancel them in your iPhone settings.

Cancel or change a plan

On your iPhone, open Settings, tap your name at the top, tap Subscriptions, choose BarbellMind, then change or cancel. Apple manages all billing.

Restore a purchase

Open the BarbellMind app, go to the paywall, and tap Restore. Make sure you are signed in to the same Apple ID you bought with.

Check your status

Enter your account email to look up your current subscription status.
